Learn is a new type of education platform.

Call us crazy, but we think education should be 100% focused on outcomes. The Learn-Verified Web Developer program is not designed to be easy. It's not for dipping your toe in the water and it's not for dabbling in new skills. There are plenty of resources online to help you do that. Learn is designed to help you change your life.

With Learn you will build real software using the same tools and workflows professional software developers use every day. Our market-aligned curriculum, provided by NYC’s Flatiron School, has already launched hundreds of careers. The material on Learn requires a tremendous amount of focus and dedication. Rather than make it easy, we connect you with a community of Learners with whom you will struggle, learn and succeed.

This won’t be easy, but you’ve got this.

Learning to code well enough to get a job as a software developer is going to be tough. Anyone telling you otherwise is, quite simply, lying. But we built Learn to make it possible for you to change your life—to launch a new career. Here's what we expect you to bring to the table:

  • Be Dedicated

    Commit some time every day to making progress on Learn.

  • Pursue Mastery

    Understand and be a master of your own code and craft.

  • Be Helpful

    This is a community. Participate generously.

  • Work Hard

    Put in the hours. Don't give up.

If you're willing to do that all that, we’ll guarantee that your commitment and hard work will pay off, and land you a job. We’re staking our reputation on it.

Job Placement Support Guarantee.

We believe so strongly in outcomes-driven education and our students’ ability to succeed that we are guaranteeing everyone who makes it through the verification process will receive a job offer within 6 months of completion or be eligible for a full refund.

Our Job Placement services include:

  • 1-on-1 job coaching from the same team that has placed hundreds of graduates from NYC’s Flatiron School into jobs as software developers throughout the world

  • Technical and cultural interview training

  • Cover letter and resume review

  • Help in identifying and applying for jobs

  • Interview pipeline management

  • Professional networking support

Here's what's required of students participating in the job placement process:
  • You complete the Learn Verified program
  • You are legally able to work in the USA
  • You are above the age of 18
  • You proactively and professionally engage with employers to whom we introduce you
  • You remain engaged in the job search process by applying for jobs, attending interviews and keeping us abreast of your progress on a weekly basis

Using Learn will feel different

Learn is not like other online learning platforms. That's because Learn was built in the classroom. Core to the experience are the things that make in-person learning so effective: learning as part of a community, working on real projects and interacting with people real-time.

Real Tools

Your can't learn real skills without real tools. We don't believe in contrived environments or multiple-choice quizzes. Learn users set up a real development environment and work through test-driven labs with a git-based workflow.

Real Time Chat

When you're stuck, you’ll get help. Right away. No need to wait a week to meet with your mentor. No emails to send. No forums to post to. Be part of a community of active Learners and Experts with whom you can work to solve problems and build software.

Learn at Your Own Pace

Move as fast as you'd like. We're here supporting you, making sure you're hitting your goals. You're not held back by an artificial schedule. Whenever you're learning, whatever your progress, you can expect other Learners to be online with you, learning together.

Open Curriculum

The curriculum on Learn is entirely open-sourced on GitHub. It's been consumed by hundreds of studetns across dozens of classes and is continually improved with feedback from students, instructors, employers, and the community at large.

Transparent Work History via GitHub.

All of your work—by which we mean every lesson you’ve ever completed on Learn—ends up on GitHub. No badges. No certificates. No degrees. Instead, a fully-transparent record of your work in a way that actually matters to employers.
